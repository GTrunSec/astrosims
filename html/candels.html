The CANDELS Survey

About

CANDELS (Cosmic Assembly Near-infrared Deep Extragalactic Legacy Survey) is a powerful imaging survey of the distant Universe being carried out with two cameras on board the Hubble Space Telescope.

For more detailed information about the project, please visit this link.

Specifications

Field Area (arcmin^2)
COSMOS 17 x 41
EGS 17 x 46
GOODS-N 32 x 32
GOODS-S 39 x 41
UDS 36 x 35

Models

Each model contains the five fields mentioned above, and eight realizations of each field, leading to a total of fourty runs per model.
